# UI Sound Design — OpenPeon Sound Pack

A designed UI sound effects pack built from [nezuai's UI Sound Design](https://freesound.org/people/nezuai/packs/32399/) on Freesound.

## Sound style

Synthetic, designed UI effects — whooshes, beeps, sweeps, thuds, and buzzes. Has a futuristic/sci-fi feel with good variety across categories.

## Category mapping

| CESP Category | Sounds | Style |
|---|---|---|
| session.start | 2 | Swelling/intense whooshes |
| task.acknowledge | 2 | Short buzz and decaying beep |
| task.complete | 2 | Positive whoosh and neutral beep |
| task.error | 3 | Low thud, negative beep, high negative whoosh |
| input.required | 2 | High whoosh and long high beep |
| resource.limit | 2 | Warning sweep and low warning tone |
| user.spam | 4 | Low buzzes, dismissive whooshes, long low beep |

17 sounds across all 7 CESP categories.

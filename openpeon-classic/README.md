# Classic (Worms) — OpenPeon Sound Pack

A Worms-style British soldier voice pack for [PeonPing](https://github.com/PeonPing/peon-ping).

## Voice style

Cheeky, over-the-top British military character — the classic Worms announcer voice. Includes battle cries, taunts, pain grunts, and victory shouts.

## Categories

| CESP Category    | Sounds |
|------------------|--------|
| session.start    | 3      |
| task.acknowledge | 5      |
| task.complete    | 9      |
| task.error       | 15     |
| input.required   | 4      |
| resource.limit   | 4      |
| user.spam        | 6      |

8 additional unmapped sounds (weapon/movement FX) are included in `sounds/` for future use.

## Installation

Copy or symlink `openpeon-classic/` into your PeonPing packs directory.
